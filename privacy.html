Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Who we are

Polario is operated by Lirium AI, a trading name of Challenger Corporation USA, S.A. ("we," "us," "our"). We are the data controller for the personal information we process through the Polario service.

2. Information we collect

When you use Polario without an account, we collect a browser fingerprint for the sole purpose of enforcing daily free usage limits. This fingerprint is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

When you create an account, we collect your email address and name (provided via Google OAuth or magic link) for account management and communication purposes.

When you purchase credits, our payment partner (the merchant of record) processes your payment information. We do not directly collect or store credit card numbers, billing addresses, or other payment details.

3. Images you upload

Images you upload to Polario are processed on secure servers for the sole purpose of providing the image cleaning service you requested. We do not view, store, share, sell, or use your images for any other purpose, including AI model training. All uploaded and processed images are automatically and permanently deleted within 48 hours of processing.

4. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to provide and maintain the Polario service, process your credit purchases, enforce usage limits and our terms of service, communicate with you about your account, and improve the service.

5. Content moderation

We use automated systems to scan uploaded images for prohibited content (such as explicit or illegal material). This scanning is performed by AI models during processing. Flagged content is rejected and not stored. We log metadata about rejected uploads (timestamp, user identifier, rejection reason) for abuse prevention purposes, but we do not retain the rejected images themselves.

6. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate Polario:

Clerk — for authentication. Their privacy policy governs how they handle your login data.

Replicate — for AI image processing. Images are sent to their servers for processing and are automatically deleted from their servers within one hour.

Our payment partner (merchant of record) — for payment processing. They handle all payment data in accordance with their own privacy policy and PCI DSS requirements.

Vercel — for hosting. Standard web server logs may be collected.

7. Data retention

Uploaded and processed images: automatically deleted within 48 hours. Account information: retained as long as your account is active. Usage logs: retained for up to 90 days. Anonymous fingerprints: reset daily.

8. Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Since images are auto-deleted within 48 hours, no action is needed for image deletion.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), server-side processing with no client-side exposure of sensitive data, automated deletion schedules, and access controls on all systems.

10. Children

Polario is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email.

12. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at team@lirium.ai.
